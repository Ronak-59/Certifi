Features

Main Features of CertiFi

CertiFi uses cryptography, blockchain(Tron) and IPFS technology to provide a secure, seamless and end-to-end credential management solution.

Low Cost

Transaction fee < $1 per issuance

Fast

Instantly issue/revoke/verify a credential.

Privacy and Security

Issued credentials can only be viewed by the recipient and recipient authorized users.

Immutable

Credentials issued on CertiFi are tamper-proof and immutable.

FAQ

Any Questions? Answered

You can contact us at headoffice@duck.com and we will be happy to answer your questions.

CertiFi App currently uses Tron Blockchain (Shasta Testnet) for demo purposes. However, the production solutions being used by the universities and clients uses different chains based on specific requirements.
To use the CertiFi app demo, you'll need to install TronLink Wallet in your browser and have an account with some test TRX on Shasta network to pay transaction fee to issue/revoke credential. TRX not needed to view/verify/share a credential. Make sure your TronLink wallet is connected and the network is "Shasta Testnet".
